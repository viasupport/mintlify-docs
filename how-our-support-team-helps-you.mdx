## Where the Line Is Drawn

To protect your privacy and maintain a clean security posture, support **will not**:

- Sign in to your VPS or any downstream systems
- Install or remove applications on your behalf
- Edit configuration files, firewall rules, or open ports
- Reset passwords or alter user roles within your instance
- Move customer data between servers or accounts

EDBB delivers the infrastructure; **you remain the administrator** of the operating system itself.
