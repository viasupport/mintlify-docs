# Protecting Your VPS Data

<Info>
  EDBB keeps the hypervisor stable but does <strong>not</strong> ship VPS plans with automatic snapshots or backups. You decide how, where, and how often to protect your own data.
</Info>

## Build a Backup Routine

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your tooling">
    Decide between snapshot-like full images (<code>dd</code>, <code>qemu-img</code>), file-level backups (<code>tar</code>, <code>rsync</code>), or sync tools (<code>rclone</code>, <code>restic</code>).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automate the cadence">
    Use cron jobs or orchestration pipelines so backups run daily or weekly without manual steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and rotate">
    Test restores periodically and prune old archives so you always have a clean, recent copy.
  </Step>
</Steps>
