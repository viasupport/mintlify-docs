EDBB prioritizes infrastructure stability, but by design we **do not include snapshot or backup services** for VPS plans built on KVM virtualization.  
That means you control how, where, and when your data is protected.

### Why Routine Backups Matter

Hardware issues, accidental deletions, and security incidents happen.  
With a reliable backup strategy, you can restore quickly, limit downtime, and avoid permanent data loss.

Our guide, [Backup and Restore Data on Linux](https://docs.edbb.com/backup-restore-data-linux), covers practical approaches including `tar`, `rsync`, `dd`, and `rclone` for off-site storage. Follow the step-by-step instructions that fit your tooling preferences.
