## Locate Your Support PIN

<Info>
  The Support PIN proves account ownership when you contact EDBB about billing,
  security, or sensitive service changes. Treat it like any other secret: share it
  only with trusted teammates and rotate it if you suspect exposure.
</Info>

---

## Spot the PIN on the Summary Card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your VPS summary">
    Sign in to the <a href="https://my.edbb.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">EDBB VPS Portal</a> and open the VPS from <strong>Services</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the right-side overview">
    The <strong>Summary</strong> card on the right lists the Support PIN alongside the KVM ID, plan, and installed OS. Tap the copy icon to grab it instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Store it safely">
    Paste the value into your password manager or internal secret vault‚Äîavoid plain-text notes or chat messages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
