# Reinstall from the Installation Tab

<Info>
  Reinstalling wipes the entire VPS disk and replaces the operating system. Double-check that off-site backups or snapshots exist before you continue.
</Info>

## Run AutoInstall in Five Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your VPS in the portal">
    Sign in to the <a href="https://my.edbb.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank">EDBB VPS Portal</a>, go to <strong>Services</strong>, and choose the VPS you want to rebuild.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Installation → AutoInstall OS">
    Use the <strong>Installation</strong> tab on the service dashboard, then stay on the default <strong>AutoInstall OS</strong> view.
    <div style={{marginTop: "16px"}}>
      <img src="/assets/vps-reinstall-os.png" alt="Installation tab in the EDBB portal" style={{width: "100%", maxWidth: "820px", borderRadius: "16px", border: "1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.08)"}} />
    </div>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the operating system">
    Click the tile for the OS family you need (Ubuntu, Debian, Rocky, AlmaLinux, Windows Server, etc.).
    <div style={{marginTop: "16px"}}>
      <img src="/assets/vps-reinstall-os-sel.png" alt="Selecting an AutoInstall template" style={{width: "100%", maxWidth: "820px", borderRadius: "16px", border: "1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.08)"}} />
    </div>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the version & confirm options">
    Use the dropdown beneath the tile to pick the release you want. If the OS offers additional options (desktop vs. minimal, language packs, etc.) review them before you proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set credentials and launch">
    Enter the new password (or paste an SSH public key) and click <strong>Start installation</strong>. The portal queues the reinstall and shows the progress log in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  No confirmation email is sent after a reinstall. Store the new credentials immediately—the password replaces the previous root/Administrator login as soon as the job completes.
</Callout>
