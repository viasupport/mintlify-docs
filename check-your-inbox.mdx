### What's Next?

After ordering and receiving a payment confirmation, please keep an eye on your mailbox for a **Welcome Email** from [**support@edbb.com**](mailto:support@edbb.com). This email contains everything you need to get started with your Virtual Private Server (VPS).

### What's in the Email?

- Your VPS Primary **IPv4 address**
- **Username **and**password** 
- **Operating system**¬†and configuration details
- A direct link to the **management dashboard**
- Quick links to to this [knowledge base](https://docs.edbb.com) and our [Acceptable Use Policy](https://docs.edbb.com/acceptable-use-policy).
- **Link to our **public [Statuspage](https://status.edbb.com).

### Didn't Receive the Email?

Check your spam folder and whitelist `support@edbb.com`. If nothing arrives within a few minutes, [follow this guide](https://docs.edbb.com/no-email-received) for troubleshooting steps.
