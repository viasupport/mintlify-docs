## Add an Invoice Recipient

1. Log in to the client portal.  
2. Click your name â†’ **Contacts / Sub-Accounts**.  
3. Choose **Add New Contact**.  
4. Enter the contact details and tick **Invoice Emails** (plus any other categories they should receive).  
5. Save the contact.

Invoices will now be sent to both the primary account email and the new contact.  
You can edit or remove contacts at any time from the same menu.
