## Add Recipients in the Portal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Contacts / Sub-Accounts">
    Sign in, click your name in the top-right corner, and choose <strong>Contacts / Sub-Accounts</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the contact">
    Select <strong>Add New Contact</strong>, fill in their name and email, and tick <strong>Invoice Emails</strong> (plus any other notifications they should receive).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and confirm">
    Save the contact. Future invoices now go to both the primary address and the new recipient. Edit or remove recipients from the same menu anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>
