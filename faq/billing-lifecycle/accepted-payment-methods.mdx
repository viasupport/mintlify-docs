## Credit and Debit Cards

Processed via Stripe with one-off or recurring billing support:  
- Visa  
- Mastercard  
- American Express  
- China UnionPay  
- Discover & Diners  
- JCB

## Cryptocurrency

EDBB accepts payments through CoinPayments and Coinbase Commerce. Supported assets include (but are not limited to):  
- Bitcoin (on-chain and Lightning)  
- Bitcoin Cash  
- Ethereum  
- Litecoin  
- USD Coin, USDT  
- Dogecoin  
- Polygon, Solana, BNB, and additional major coins

## Digital Wallets

- Apple Pay  
- Google Pay  
- Alipay

Payments post instantly to your account once confirmed by the processor. Contact Support if you need assistance with a specific method.
