## Download Invoices in Seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invoice list">
    Log in to the <a href="https://my.edbb.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">EDBB VPS Portal</a> and navigate to <strong>Billing â†’ My Invoices</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the document">
    Use search or filters (status, date range, amount) to locate the invoice you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export the PDF">
    Open the invoice and click <strong>Download PDF</strong> for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Invoices are emailed to the primary account address automatically. Add teammates under <strong>Contacts / Sub-Accounts</strong> if they also need copies.
