## Portal Access

1. Log in to the [EDBB client portal](https://my.edbb.com/clientarea.php).  
2. Click **Billing â†’ My Invoices**.  
3. Use the search or filters to find the invoice you need.  
4. Open the invoice and select **Download PDF**.

Invoices are also emailed to your primary contact.  
Add additional recipients under **Contacts** if you need copies sent elsewhere.
