## Eligibility Checklist

You may request a refund when **all** of the following are true:

- VPS ordered within the past **24 hours**  
- No discount codes, special deals, traffic add-ons, or plan upgrades were applied  
- No prior refund granted in the last **12 months**  
- Service was not suspended for AUP violations

If you meet the criteria, cancel the VPS with immediate effect and follow the instructions in the [Cancellation & Refund Policy](./cancellation-and-refund-policy) to submit your request with the IP address and Support PIN.
