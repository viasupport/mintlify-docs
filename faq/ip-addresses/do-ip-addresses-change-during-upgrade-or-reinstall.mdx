## What Happens to Your IPs?

<Info>
  Reinstalling the operating system or changing your VPS plan does <strong>not</strong> rotate the assigned IPv4 or IPv6. The addresses stay linked to the service for as long as it remains active.
</Info>

---

- Adding secondary IPs appends new addresses but leaves the primary untouched.
