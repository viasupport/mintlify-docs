## Free Change Within 24 Hours

Every new VPS includes a one-time IP change at no cost during the first 24 hours.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the EDBB VPS Portal">
    Sign in to the <a href="https://my.edbb.com/clientarea.php" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">EDBB VPS Portal</a> and pick the VPS from <strong>Services â†’ My Products</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay on the Dashboard tab">
    The summary card on the right lists <strong>Primary IPv4</strong> with copy and refresh icons. This is where the free swap is triggered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request the change">
    Click the circular arrows icon beside the IP, confirm the prompt, and the portal will queue the replacement address.
  </Step>
</Steps>
