## Availability

EDBB does **not** announce third-party IP ranges for VPS instances.  \n+If you need to advertise your own prefix, migrate the workload to an EDBB dedicated server.

## Dedicated Server Requirements

- One-time NRC for BGP announcement: **USD 250**  \n
- No recurring fee for maintaining the route advertisement  \n
- Prefix must comply with all EDBB policies, including the prohibition on unsolicited email

Submit a ticket through the portal with your ASN, LOA, and prefix details to begin the process.
